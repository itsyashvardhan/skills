# Client Communication

Write clear, professional messages that preserve trust and make next steps obvious.

## Workflow

1. Identify recipient, relationship, context, and desired outcome.
2. Choose the right tone: concise, accountable, calm, and specific.
3. Put the main point early.
4. Include relevant context, decisions needed, owner, timeline, and next action.
5. Be explicit about risks or blockers without overexplaining internal details.
6. Remove ambiguity before sending.

## Style

- Prefer plain language over corporate filler.
- Do not over-apologize.
- Do not promise timelines or scope that are not confirmed.
- For difficult messages, acknowledge impact and offer a concrete path forward.
