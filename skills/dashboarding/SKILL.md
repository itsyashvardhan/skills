# Dashboarding

Design dashboards that answer recurring questions quickly.

## Workflow

1. Identify audience, decisions, update cadence, and source data.
2. Define metrics before choosing charts.
3. Put the most important status and trend signals first.
4. Use comparisons, targets, and time windows where they add meaning.
5. Keep filters predictable and avoid duplicating similar charts.
6. Call out stale data, missing data, and metric caveats.

## Quality Bar

Every tile or chart should answer a specific question.
