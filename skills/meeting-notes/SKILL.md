# Meeting Notes

Transform raw meeting content into useful records and follow-ups.

## Workflow

1. Identify meeting purpose, participants, date, and context when available.
2. Extract decisions, action items, owners, deadlines, risks, blockers, and open questions.
3. Preserve important nuance, but remove filler and repetition.
4. Separate objective notes from interpretation.
5. Highlight follow-ups that require confirmation.

## Default Format

- Summary
- Decisions
- Action Items
- Risks or Blockers
- Open Questions
- Notes

Use a shorter format for quick recaps and a fuller format for formal records.
